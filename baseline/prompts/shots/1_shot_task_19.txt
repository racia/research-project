You will be given a sequence of context sentences and then asked questions about them.
Please answer the questions in one word.

*EXAMPLE*
Context sentences:
1. The kitchen is west of the bathroom.
2. The hallway is west of the bedroom.
3. The garden is north of the office.
4. The bathroom is west of the office.
5. The bedroom is north of the bathroom.
Question:
6. How do you go from the bedroom to the office?
Reasoning step by step:
- The bedroom is north of the bathroom. So, to go from the bedroom to the bathroom, you go south.
- The bathroom is west of the office. So, to go from the bathroom to the office, you go east.
Chain of thought step by step:
- To go from the bedroom to the office, you go south and then east.
Answer: south, east
